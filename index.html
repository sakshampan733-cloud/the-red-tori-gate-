IC

One IC card each

  • Buy a physical ICOCA per person at Kansai Airport on arrival — it works on every subway, bus & local JR line in all three cities (the cards are nationwide now).
  • Keep it as a souvenir — valid 10 years, balance never expires. No refund hassle, use it again next trip.
  • Top up in small amounts near the end so you leave only a tiny balance behind.
  • IC does not cover the Shinkansen or the Odakyu Romancecar — buy those tickets separately.
宅

Luggage by courier

  • Use takkyūbin (Yamato) once: send the big bags Kyoto hotel → Tokyo hotel on the morning of Aug 9. They arrive next day.
  • You then do the whole Fuji day with just daypacks — no suitcases in the van or on the Shinkansen.
  • Limit: 25 kg and 160 cm (L+W+H) per bag — a normal large suitcase is fine. Keep mid-trip bags lighter; save heavy packing for the flight home.
得

Cheat codes

  • Skip the JR Pass — only ~2 long trips, so single tickets are cheaper.
  • Book the Fuji-day van 3–4 weeks ahead (peak August) via Klook / KKday — give them the exact route (Odawara → Shiraito → Oishi → Shinjuku) and confirm a fixed day price, not metered.
  • The Odakyu Enoshima-Kamakura Freepass covers the beach day's trains from Shinjuku, including the Enoden.
  • Google Maps gives live platforms, times and fares — it's flawless in Japan.
